Longevity

To ensure long-term durability and longevity, our covers are reinforced with extra layers of material underneath the vulnerable areas to prevent wear and tear. Additionally, all edges of the cover have binding to prevent tearing, reduce rips, and protect against damage, thereby providing the cover with extra strength.

Protection

We strive to make all of our covers as watertight as possible. To achieve this, we carefully consider how much we can overlap the sides and bottom of the boat to provide the best possible protection against the elements.

Material Quality

At our company, we prioritize the use of only the highest quality materials available in the market, sourced from leading manufacturers. We strongly believe in never compromising on quality, as it is essential for the longevity and performance of our covers.

Fittings & Framework

We utilize only the highest quality aluminium or stainless-steel bars available in the market, ranging from 19-25mm O.D. depending on the specific project requirements. This provides a secure foundation to support the canvas and ensure optimal performance. For larger projects such as wide beams, we use 25mm bars to guarantee stability and longevity.
