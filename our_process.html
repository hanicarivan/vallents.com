CONSULTATION

Our initial interaction with clients involves a consultation, which can be conducted via phone, email or in-person at our offices. During this consultation, we will carefully evaluate your product specifications, offer insight into the array of materials and colours available, as well as discuss customizable options such as zips, windows, and any other variables that may align with your specific requirements.

MEASURING

Our company boasts more than four decades of experience in measuring by means of the traditional approach of creating templates utilizing patterning materials. However, we have significantly enhanced our accuracy and precision by investing in state-of-the-art 3D measuring technology. Depending on the project requirements, we can generate a precise and accurate pattern utilizing either of these methods.

DESIGN

We tailor our project design approach based on the chosen measuring technique. This involves utilizing 3D CAD software or hand-designing by one of our artists. Regardless of the approach, we take great care to ensure that the final result is aesthetically pleasing and aligns with your preferences.

LASER CUTTING

All of our projects undergo precise cutting to match their measuring pattern, utilizing the advanced DYSS X7 digital laser cutter machine.

SEWING

All of our highly skilled seamstresses are qualified professionals who use industrial sewing machines to ensure neat and accurate stitching for your product.

INSTALMENT

Our team of extensively trained fitters will perform the installation and fitting of all projects. Upon completion, they will provide a thorough demonstration of any features that are included with your project.
